Frequently Asked Questions About Adventure

What is the cost of enrolling my child in Camp Kinder Ring’s summer adventure programs?
Our standard summer session runs for 7 weeks and tuition for 2024 is $9,800, which includes all activities, meals, and lodging at our Hopewell Junction camp. We also offer shorter mini-sessions and sibling discounts. Please visit our website or contact our office for the most up-to-date pricing and available session dates.
How early should I register for a spot at Camp Kinder Ring, and is there a waitlist?
We recommend registering as early as possible, as our adventure programs fill up quickly—often by early winter for the following summer. If a specific session is full, you can join our waitlist, and we’ll contact you if a spot becomes available. Registration opens each September for the next summer season.
Is Camp Kinder Ring licensed and inspected for safety in New York State?
Yes, Camp Kinder Ring is fully licensed by the New York State Department of Health and undergoes annual inspections to ensure compliance with all health and safety regulations. Our staff are certified in first aid, CPR, and wilderness safety, and we maintain rigorous safety protocols for all adventure activities.
What is the daily schedule like, and how are adventure activities organized?
Campers experience a structured yet flexible daily schedule that includes a mix of adventure activities such as zip lining, hiking, boating on Sylvan Lake, and ropes courses. Activities are organized by age group and skill level, with experienced counselors guiding each group. Campers also have opportunities to choose electives and participate in special events unique to the Hopewell Junction area.
What happens if my child is unhappy or needs to leave camp early? Is there a refund policy?
We strive to support every camper’s comfort and happiness at Camp Kinder Ring. If a child needs to leave early for medical or family reasons, we review refund requests on a case-by-case basis, outlined in our enrollment agreement. Our team will work closely with your family to resolve any issues, and we offer on-site support to help campers adjust and enjoy their experience.
